At Shrishti, excellence is a way of life and printing comes naturally.

With imported printing machines for multicolor printing that can
handle paper sizes from 5” x 7” to 28” x 40” and state-of-the-art
cutting, wire stitching and lamination machines that ensure a
perfect finish, Shrishti has pre-press facilities like scanning,
pagination and color correction as well as post press works like
die-cutting, folding, binding, embossing, foil and UV coating,
to name a few.

A high-class 4000 square feet infrastructure backed by continuous
power back up, a team of skilled staff, great value-added services
like screen printing and door-step deliveries make Shrishti,
the preferred one-stop for all your printing requirements.

Shrishti has planned outside-the-city services and pan-India
services in the near future.
